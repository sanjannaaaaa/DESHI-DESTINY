KARNATAKA

Bangalore (Bengaluru): The capital city known for its vibrant culture, gardens like Lalbagh Botanical Garden, historical sites like Bangalore Palace, and modern landmarks like Vidhana Soudha. Mysore: Famous for the magnificent Mysore Palace, vibrant Dasara festival, and Chamundi Hill offering panoramic views of the city. Coorg (Kodagu): Known as the "Scotland of India," it offers lush greenery, coffee plantations, trekking trails, and attractions like Abbey Falls and Dubare Elephant Camp. Hampi: A UNESCO World Heritage Site renowned for its ancient ruins, temples, and monuments belonging to the Vijayanagara Empire. Gokarna: A coastal town with pristine beaches like Om Beach and Kudle Beach, as well as temples and trekking opportunities. Badami: Famous for its rock-cut cave temples, historical monuments, and beautiful Agastya Lake. Chikmagalur: Known for its coffee plantations, scenic beauty, and attractions like Mullayanagiri Peak and Baba Budangiri Hills. Bandipur National Park: A wildlife sanctuary known for its diverse flora and fauna, offering opportunities for safaris and nature walks.
