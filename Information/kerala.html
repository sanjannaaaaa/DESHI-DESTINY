KERALA

Kochi (Cochin): Known for its Chinese fishing nets, Fort Kochi, Mattancherry Palace, and vibrant Jewish Synagogue. Munnar: A picturesque hill station known for its tea plantations, Eravikulam National Park, and Anamudi Peak. Alleppey (Alappuzha): Famous for its backwaters, houseboat cruises, Alleppey Beach, and Nehru Trophy Boat Race. Kumarakom: A serene backwater destination known for bird watching, Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary, and traditional houseboat stays. Thekkady: Home to Periyar National Park, offering wildlife safaris, boating in Periyar Lake, and spice plantations. Kovalam: Known for its beautiful beaches, lighthouse, water sports, and Ayurvedic treatments. Wayanad: A lush green hill station offering trekking, wildlife sanctuaries, waterfalls like Meenmutty and Soochipara, and Edakkal Caves. Trivandrum (Thiruvananthapuram): The capital city known for its Padmanabhaswamy Temple, Napier Museum, Kuthira Malika Palace, and beautiful beaches like Kovalam and Varkala.
