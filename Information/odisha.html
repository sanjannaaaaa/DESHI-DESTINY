Puri: Famous for the Jagannath Temple, a major Hindu pilgrimage site, and the annual Rath Yatra festival, along with the golden beaches of Puri Beach. Bhubaneswar: The capital city known for its ancient temples, including the Lingaraja Temple, Mukteshwar Temple, and Khandagiri and Udayagiri caves. Konark: Home to the UNESCO-listed Sun Temple, a magnificent temple dedicated to the Sun God, known for its architectural grandeur and intricate stone carvings. Chilika Lake: Asia's largest brackish water lagoon, a haven for migratory birds and home to the Irrawaddy dolphins, offering boat cruises and birdwatching. Cuttack: Known as the "Silver City," famous for its silver filigree work (Tarakasi), ancient forts, and the Barabati Fort and Mahanadi River. Raghurajpur: A heritage crafts village known for its Pattachitra paintings, traditional handicrafts, and Gotipua dance performances. Simlipal National Park: A tiger reserve and biodiversity hotspot known for its dense forests, waterfalls, and diverse flora and fauna. Gopalpur-on-Sea: A serene beach town known for its tranquil beaches, fishing villages, and colonial-era lighthouse.
