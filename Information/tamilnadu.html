TAMIL NADU

Chennai: The capital city known for Marina Beach, Kapaleeshwarar Temple, Fort St. George, and vibrant cultural scene. Madurai: Famous for the Meenakshi Amman Temple, Thirumalai Nayakkar Palace, and vibrant street markets. Mahabalipuram: A UNESCO World Heritage Site renowned for its ancient rock-cut temples, shore temple, and beach. Ooty (Udhagamandalam): A popular hill station offering scenic beauty, botanical gardens, tea plantations, and the Nilgiri Mountain Railway. Kodaikanal: Known for its serene lakes, picturesque landscapes, Coaker's Walk, and Bryant Park. Rameswaram: A sacred pilgrimage site with the Ramanathaswamy Temple, Pamban Bridge, and pristine beaches. Thanjavur: Famous for the Brihadeeswarar Temple, Thanjavur Palace, and art treasures. Kanyakumari: The southernmost tip of India, known for Vivekananda Rock Memorial, Thiruvalluvar Statue, and stunning sunrises and sunsets.
