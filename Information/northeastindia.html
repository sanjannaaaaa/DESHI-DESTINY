NORTH EAST INDIA

Northeast India is a region known for its stunning natural beauty, diverse culture, and rich history. Here are some of the top tourist places in Northeast India: Kaziranga National Park, Assam: Famous for its population of endangered one-horned rhinoceroses, Kaziranga is also home to tigers, elephants, and various bird species. It's a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Tawang, Arunachal Pradesh: Known for its ancient monasteries, including the Tawang Monastery, which is the largest in India. Tawang also offers breathtaking Himalayan landscapes and is an important pilgrimage site for Buddhists. Shillong, Meghalaya: Often referred to as the "Scotland of the East," Shillong is known for its picturesque landscapes, including waterfalls like Elephant Falls and the living root bridges of Cherrapunji. Majuli, Assam: The largest river island in the world, Majuli is known for its rich culture, Vaishnavite monasteries (Satras), and vibrant festivals. Gangtok, Sikkim: The capital city of Sikkim, Gangtok offers stunning views of the Himalayas, serene monasteries like Rumtek, and adventure activities like trekking and river rafting.
