GOA

Beaches: Explore the famous beaches like Baga, Calangute, Anjuna, and Palolem, known for their golden sands, water sports, and lively shacks. Old Goa: Visit the UNESCO World Heritage Site, which houses several ancient churches and cathedrals, including the Basilica of Bom Jesus and Se Cathedral. Dudhsagar Falls: Experience the majestic beauty of one of India's tallest waterfalls, surrounded by lush greenery and accessible by trekking or jeep safari. Fort Aguada: Explore this well-preserved 17th-century Portuguese fort, offering panoramic views of the Arabian Sea and the surrounding landscape. Dolphin Watching: Embark on a boat trip to spot playful dolphins in their natural habitat in the Arabian Sea. Spice Plantations: Take a tour of spice plantations to learn about Goa's rich agricultural heritage and sample local spices and traditional Goan cuisine. Cruise on Mandovi River: Enjoy a leisurely cruise along the Mandovi River, offering views of Goa's scenic coastline, lively villages, and sunset vistas.
